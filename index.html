Cups of Coffee

Featured Shop!

Our month's featured shop is Big Mug Coffee Roaster in Santa Clara, California.

This shop is a cozy Korean cafe with hanging Edison bulbs and a chalk map of coffee around the world. 

Recommended drinks: Mint au Lait, Matcha Latte and Latte. 

This cute spot is open late with plenty of tables, studying people, and an easy going ambience. 

Perfect for studying or dates!
